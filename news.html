Under 16 South East Soccer Cup
Posted: 16 October 2017
Well done to our U16 soccer team that defeated St Paul's today in the South East Cup. After falling behind after only 1 minute of play our boys showed great character to level the game early in the second half and taking the game right to St Paul's from then on. The game went to extra time and penalties with Mount Sion progressing after 5 successful spot kicks. Well done lads!
